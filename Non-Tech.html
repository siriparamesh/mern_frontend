Non-Technical Clubs

Music Club

The Music Club is a club run by the students of the institute. A paradise for musicians, the club is responsible for some of the hottest events on campus. Our ultimate objective is to keep the music culture at our university alive, no matter the workload. After all, music is life.

Cultural Club of the institute is a platform for all the cultural activities like singing,dancing,arts and crafts.We make the events of the institute beautiful.
